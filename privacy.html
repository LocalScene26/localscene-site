Privacy Policy Last updated: April 2026 LocalScene ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. 1. Information We Collect We collect the following information when you use LocalScene: Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account. Profile Information: Profile photos, musical interests, instruments, genres, and other details you choose to add to your profile. Location Data: Your location to show you nearby musicians, venues, and music events. Location is collected only while using the app and with your permission. Messages: Content of messages you send and receive through the app's messaging feature. Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Provide and operate the LocalScene app Connect you with nearby musicians and music events Enable messaging between users Display relevant profiles and content based on your location and preferences Process subscriptions and payments Improve and develop new features Send important service-related communications 3. How We Share Your Information We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with: Other Users: Your profile information, photos, and approximate location are visible to other LocalScene users. Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics). Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect the safety of our users. 4. Data Storage and Security We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your data is stored on secure servers. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. 5. Your Choices Location: You can disable location access through your device settings at any time. Profile: You can update or delete your profile information within the app. Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data from our active systems. 6. Children's Privacy LocalScene is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by other means. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 8. Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: legal@localscene.app
